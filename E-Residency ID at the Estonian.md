# Information Regarding Possibility of Picking Up the ID at Estonian Honorary Consulate in Islamabad, Pakistan

- **Possibility of picking up an E-Residency ID at the Estonian Honorary Consulate in Islamabad, Pakistan?**
 
There is no pickup location in Pakistan and will need to choose a pickup location in another country. Visit the link to see [List of pickup locations](https://www.e-resident.gov.ee/pick-up-locations/)
